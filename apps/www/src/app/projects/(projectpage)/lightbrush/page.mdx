# Lightbrush - Turn your flashlight into a lightpainting brush

An embedded C firmware to turn your flashlight into a lightpainting brush.

This Project is a highly customized fork of Anduril 2, aimed at turning affordable flashlights into workable light painting brushes.

The firmware allows for the use of various patterns used in lightpainting photography.
